Why Canada

Quality, co-ops, settlement

Career-aligned learning with one of the clearest PR pathways for international graduates.

Funding & visa

Scholarships, funding & the Canada visa

Two parallel tracks — how to fund the degree, and how to secure your stay.

Scholarships & funding

  • Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships for doctoral students.
  • Lester B. Pearson International Scholarship at University of Toronto.
  • Trudeau Foundation Doctoral Scholarships.
  • University-specific entrance scholarships and graduate teaching assistantships.
  • GIC + education loan combinations through major Indian lenders.

Student visa snapshot

  • Visa name: Study Permit via IRCC.
  • Requires a Letter of Acceptance from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI).
  • Proof of funds: typically CAD 20,635/year + tuition (2024 update).
  • GIC (Guaranteed Investment Certificate) of CAD 20,635 for SDS pathway.
  • Work allowance: up to 24 hours/week off-campus during term.
Timeline

Application timeline

A typical 10–14 month roadmap for a Fall intake.

Profile review & shortlist

12–14 months out: program direction, budget, target provinces.

Tests & documents

8–12 months out: IELTS/PTE; GRE/GMAT only if program requires.

Applications

October–February: most graduate programs; UG via OUAC for Ontario.

Offers & GIC

January–April: confirm offer, pay deposit, open GIC, finalise loan.

Study Permit

April–July: biometrics, medical, application via SDS or non-SDS.

Pre-departure & arrival

July–September: housing, banking, insurance, flight, campus arrival.
